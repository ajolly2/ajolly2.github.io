Is sitting really the new smoking? No.

“Sitting is more dangerous than smoking, kills more people than HIV, and is more treacherous than parachuting.
We are sitting ourselves to death.”
- Dr. James Levine, Mayo Clinic









Employers are already paying the cost of unhealthy employees






  Decreased productivity

Due to health risk factors, the average employee performs at only 70% of full capacity, costing employers half a trillion dollars each year. For every dollar spent on healthcare, a company loses up to $20 in productivity.




  Higher healthcare costs

The top 10 causes of rising workplace healthcare costs--cancer, back/neck pain, heart disease, chronic pain, high cholesterol, depression, obesity, arthritis, and anxiety--have one common cause: long periods of sitting.
